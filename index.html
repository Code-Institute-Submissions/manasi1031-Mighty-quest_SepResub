About Us


Welcome to this free website where children can learn while they play.
Mighty Quest is an interactive tool to help growth of children in year 2 and move towards year 3.
The website has lessons for children to read and understand with an interactive quiz related to a subject.
There is also a stress buster memory game to improve concentration.
I believe that every child will achieve their potential if they are happy, secure and have ownership of what they learn and the right learning conditions.
If you have any feedback, please contact us using the form on the "contact" page.
